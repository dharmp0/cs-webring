CS Webring

Connecting CS student personal websites

Welcome to the Webring

A webring links personal websites together in a ring. Members add a small embed snippet to their site so visitors can navigate to the previous or next member.

Member Directory

Join the Webring

Want to join? Add your site to data/members.json via Pull Request on GitHub.

Embed the Webring on Your Site

After your PR is merged, paste this HTML into your site:

<div style="display:flex;align-items:center;gap:8px;">
  <a href="https://cs-webring.pages.dev/go?site=https%3A%2F%2Fyour-site.com%2F&nav=prev">&larr;</a>
  <a href="https://cs-webring.pages.dev/">
    <img src="https://cs-webring.pages.dev/icon.black.svg" alt="CS Webring" style="width:24px;height:auto;opacity:0.8;" />
  </a>
  <a href="https://cs-webring.pages.dev/go?site=https%3A%2F%2Fyour-site.com%2F&nav=next">&rarr;</a>
</div>

Replace https://your-site.com/ with your actual submitted site URL (URL-encoded in the site= parameter).
